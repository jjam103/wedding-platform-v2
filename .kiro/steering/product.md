# Product Overview

## Costa Rica Wedding Management System

A comprehensive destination wedding management platform designed for multi-owner wedding coordination in Costa Rica. The system manages the complete wedding experience from guest invitations through post-wedding photo sharing.

### Core Purpose
- **Guest Portal**: Email-based login, RSVP management, accommodation details, itinerary access
- **Admin Dashboard**: Complete wedding coordination including guest management, activity planning, vendor tracking, and budget oversight
- **Multi-Owner Support**: Family-based guest grouping with shared access and management capabilities

### Key Features

#### Activity & Event Management
- **Event Creation**: Multi-day wedding events with dates, times, locations, and hierarchical organization
- **Activity Types**: Predefined types (ceremony, reception, meal, transport, activity) plus custom activity types
- **Capacity Management**: Per-activity capacity limits with real-time tracking and 90% threshold alerts
- **Cost Tracking**: Activity-level costs with per-guest pricing and host subsidy calculations
- **Access Controls**: Adults-only restrictions and plus-one limitations per activity
- **Rich Content**: Activity descriptions with rich text editing and photo attachments
- **Scheduling**: Drag-and-drop activity reordering and conflict detection
- **Itinerary Generation**: Personalized guest itineraries with PDF export capabilities

#### RSVP System
- **Dual-Layer RSVPs**: Event-level attendance tracking plus individual activity-level RSVPs
- **Response States**: Four-state tracking (attending, declined, maybe, pending)
- **Guest Count Tracking**: Accurate headcount with plus-one support and guest count specifications
- **Dietary Restrictions**: Per-guest dietary requirements and special needs tracking
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Response rate calculations, capacity utilization, and attendance forecasting
- **Automated Notifications**: RSVP confirmations, deadline reminders, and capacity alerts
- **Deadline Management**: RSVP deadline enforcement with automated reminder emails to non-respondents

#### Accommodation Management
- **3-Tier Hierarchy**: Accommodations → room types → guest assignments
- **Pricing & Subsidies**: Per-night costs with host subsidy tracking and automatic calculations
- **Capacity Tracking**: Room availability monitoring and occupancy management
- **Location Integration**: Hierarchical location relationships linking accommodations to venues
- **Booking Status**: Confirmation tracking and assignment change management

#### Photo Gallery
- **Moderated Uploads**: Three-state workflow (pending, approved, rejected) for guest submissions
- **Multiple Display Modes**: Gallery grid, carousel, and auto-play loop configurations
- **Dual Storage**: Cost-optimized CDN delivery with Backblaze B2 and Supabase Storage
- **Batch Operations**: Multi-photo uploads with metadata (captions, alt text, attribution)
- **Page Organization**: Photos organized by events, activities, and custom pages

#### Email Automation
- **Template System**: Variable substitution for personalized communications
- **Automated Triggers**: RSVP confirmations, activity reminders, deadline notifications
- **Bulk & Individual**: Mass email campaigns and targeted individual messages
- **Delivery Tracking**: Real-time webhook integration for delivery status and engagement analytics
- **Email Scheduling**: Future-dated email delivery with queue management

#### Budget Tracking
- **Vendor Management**: Categorized vendors (photography, catering, music, transportation, etc.)
- **Pricing Models**: Flat-rate and per-guest pricing with automatic calculations
- **Payment Tracking**: Three-state payment status (unpaid, partial, paid) with balance tracking
- **Subsidy Calculations**: Host subsidies and guest contribution tracking across activities and accommodations
- **Real-Time Summaries**: Automatic budget totals with cost breakdowns and export capabilities

#### Content Management
- **Dynamic Pages**: Custom content pages with rich text editing and reference linking
- **Section System**: Two-column layouts with rich text, photo galleries, and cross-references
- **Version History**: Page snapshots with revert capabilities
- **Reference Validation**: Circular reference detection and broken link checking

#### Transportation Coordination
- **Flight Tracking**: Arrival/departure times for SJO, LIR, and other airports
- **Manifest Generation**: Automated arrival and departure manifests by date
- **Shuttle Coordination**: Guest-to-shuttle assignments with vehicle requirement calculations
- **Driver Sheets**: Printable driver sheets with guest details and pickup/dropoff locations
- **Cost Tracking**: Shuttle cost calculations based on vehicle requirements

### Target Users
- **Wedding Couples**: Primary coordinators managing their destination wedding
- **Wedding Guests**: Accessing information, submitting RSVPs, sharing photos
- **Family Coordinators**: Multi-owner access for distributed wedding planning
- **Wedding Planners**: Professional coordination tools and analytics

### Business Context
Designed specifically for destination weddings requiring complex logistics coordination, multi-family involvement, and comprehensive guest experience management. Optimized for Costa Rica wedding venues with tropical theming and local considerations.
