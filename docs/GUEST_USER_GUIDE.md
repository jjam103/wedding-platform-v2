## Authentication

### First Time Login

You'll receive an email invitation with a link to access the portal. Click the link to get started.

### Login Methods

The system supports two authentication methods:

#### Email Matching

**How it works**:
1. Go to the guest login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Continue"
4. If your email matches our records, you're logged in

**Requirements**:
- Your email must match the one in our system
- One email per guest/family

#### Magic Link

**How it works**:
1. Go to the guest login page
2. Click "Send Magic Link"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for the magic link
5. Click the link to log in

**Magic Link Details**:
- Link expires after 15 minutes
- Link can only be used once
- Request a new link if expired

### Staying Logged In

- Your session lasts 24 hours
- You'll be automatically logged out after 24 hours of inactivity
- Close your browser to log out immediately

### Troubleshooting Login

**Email not recognized**:
- Check for typos in your email
- Try the email you used when you RSVP'd
- Contact the wedding coordinators

**Magic link expired**:
- Request a new magic link
- Links expire after 15 minutes

**Magic link not working**:
- Make sure you're using the latest link
- Try copying and pasting the full URL
- Request a new link if needed

## RSVP System

### Viewing Events & Activities

**Navigation**: Events or Activities tab

**Event Information**:
- Event name and description
- Date and time
- Location
- Dress code
- Special instructions

**Activity Information**:
- Activity name and description
- Date and time
- Location
- Capacity and availability
- Cost (if applicable)
- Adults-only restriction (if applicable)

### Submitting RSVPs

**For Events**:
1. Go to Events tab
2. Click on an event
3. Select your response:
   - Attending
   - Maybe
   - Declined
4. Add guest count (if bringing plus-ones)
5. Add dietary restrictions (if applicable)
6. Click "Submit RSVP"

**For Activities**:
1. Go to Activities tab
2. Click on an activity
3. Select your response:
   - Attending
   - Maybe
   - Declined
4. Add guest count
5. Add dietary restrictions
6. Add special requirements
7. Click "Submit RSVP"

### RSVP Status

**Status Options**:
- **Pending** - You haven't responded yet
- **Attending** - You're coming!
- **Maybe** - You're not sure yet
- **Declined** - You can't make it

### Changing RSVPs

You can change your RSVP anytime before the deadline:

1. Go to the event or activity
2. Click "Change RSVP"
3. Update your response
4. Click "Update RSVP"

**Note**: Some activities have capacity limits. If an activity is full, you may be added to a waitlist.

### RSVP Deadlines

- RSVP deadlines are shown on each event/activity
- You'll receive reminder emails before deadlines
- Late RSVPs may not be accepted
- Contact coordinators if you miss a deadline

### Dietary Restrictions

**Adding Restrictions**:
1. When RSVPing, find "Dietary Restrictions" field
2. Enter your restrictions (e.g., "vegetarian", "gluten-free", "nut allergy")
3. Be specific about allergies
4. Submit with your RSVP

**Updating Restrictions**:
- You can update restrictions anytime
- Changes apply to all events/activities
- Coordinators are notified of changes

### Plus-Ones

**If plus-ones are allowed**:
1. Select "Attending"
2. Enter guest count (including yourself)
3. Add plus-one's name in notes (if requested)
4. Submit RSVP

**Note**: Not all activities allow plus-ones. Check activity details.

## Photo Gallery

**Navigation**: Photos tab

### Viewing Photos

**Gallery Displays**:
- All approved wedding photos
- Photos from coordinators
- Photos from other guests (if approved)

**Display Modes**:
- Grid view - Browse all photos
- Carousel view - Slideshow with navigation
- Auto-play - Automatic slideshow

**Photo Details**:
- Click photo to view full size
- View caption and attribution
- Download individual photos

### Uploading Photos

**Sharing Your Photos**:
1. Go to Photos tab
2. Click "Upload Photos"
3. Select files (max 10MB each)
4. Add captions (optional)
5. Click "Upload"

**Upload Guidelines**:
- Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Maximum size: 10MB per photo
- Maximum 10 photos per upload
- Photos reviewed before appearing in gallery

**Photo Moderation**:
- Your photos are reviewed by coordinators
- Approved photos appear in gallery
- You're notified when photos are approved
- Inappropriate photos may be rejected

### Downloading Photos

**Individual Photos**:
- Click photo to view full size
- Click "Download" button
- Photo saved to your device

**Bulk Download**:
1. Select multiple photos (checkboxes)
2. Click "Download Selected"
3. Photos downloaded as ZIP file

## FAQs

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password**
A: The system uses email-based authentication. Request a new magic link to log in.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact the wedding coordinators to update your email.

**Q: How long does my session last?**
A: Sessions last 24 hours. You'll be automatically logged out after that.

### RSVPs

**Q: Can I change my RSVP?**
A: Yes, you can change your RSVP anytime before the deadline.

**Q: What if I miss the RSVP deadline?**
A: Contact the wedding coordinators. Late RSVPs may not be accepted.

**Q: Can I RSVP for my family?**
A: If you're a group owner, yes. Otherwise, each person must RSVP individually.

**Q: What if an activity is full?**
A: You can join the waitlist. You'll be notified if a spot opens up.

### Itinerary

**Q: Why don't I see all events in my itinerary?**
A: Your itinerary only shows events/activities you RSVP'd "attending" to.

**Q: Can I add my itinerary to my calendar?**
A: Yes, export as iCal and import into your calendar app.

**Q: Will my itinerary update if I change RSVPs?**
A: Yes, your itinerary updates automatically when you change RSVPs.

### Photos

**Q: When will my photos appear in the gallery?**
A: Photos are reviewed by coordinators. Approved photos appear within 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I delete photos I uploaded?**
A: Contact the coordinators to request photo removal.

**Q: Can I download all photos at once?**
A: Yes, select multiple photos and click "Download Selected".

### Family Management

**Q: What is a group owner?**
A: A group owner can manage information and RSVPs for their family/group.

**Q: How do I become a group owner?**
A: Contact the wedding coordinators to request group owner status.

**Q: Can I see other groups' information?**
A: No, you can only see your own group's information.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The site isn't working on my phone**
A: Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox). Update your browser if needed.

**Q: I'm having trouble uploading photos**
A: Check file size (max 10MB) and format (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP). Try uploading fewer photos at once.

**Q: Changes aren't saving**
A: Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page. Contact support if issue persists.
